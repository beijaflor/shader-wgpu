shader-wgpu

A focused WebGPU study surface for 2D halftone shaders inspired by Shades of Halftone.

Study Flow

Loading WebGPU and preparing the first sample.

Canvas

WebGPU is required. If initialization fails, the status will explain why.

Controls

Each stage exposes only the controls needed to understand that step of the halftone construction.

What To Look For

Keep the focus on 2D image-space halftone logic: source image, luminance, grid, dots, corrected output, channel screens, and controlled imperfections.
